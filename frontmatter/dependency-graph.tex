\chapter*{How the chapters depend on each other}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Chapter dependency graph}

\noindent
The chart below tells you what to read first. A solid arrow ``\textbf{Ch.~A} $\to$ \textbf{Ch.~B}'' means \emph{you need Ch.~A before Ch.~B}. A dotted arrow means Ch.~A \emph{motivates} Ch.~B but isn't strictly required.

If you are reading the cliff-notes track, the chain Ch.~1 $\to$ Ch.~7 $\to$ Ch.~10 $\to$ Ch.~12 $\to$ Ch.~17 $\to$ Ch.~21 is the minimum-viable spine.
